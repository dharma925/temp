Yantrik

About us!

Yantrik is one of the major clubs of IIT Mandi. It comes under the Science and Technology Council. Yantrik conducts various events like junkyard wars, RC races, and even mechatronics workshop. Yantrik is growing with the contribution of new members with each coming year.

RC Nitro Car

An advanced model RC car built with similar working of a regular car with some modifications. Nitro-powered cars operate like full-sized fuel vehicles more than their electric counterparts do, making use of a two stroke engine rather than an electric motor

RC Plane

An RC plane is a simple, small flying object which is basically controlled in the air using a hand-held transmitter operated from the ground base. The main aim of this project was to design, analyses and build an RC plane.
